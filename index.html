About Us

The Brock Public School Council is a group of parents, guardians, and community members dedicated to supporting our school and enhancing the educational experience for all students.

We work collaboratively with school administration, teachers, and the broader community to provide input on school policies, organize events, and support initiatives that benefit our students and school.

Get Involved

We welcome all parents and guardians to participate in our school council. Your involvement makes a difference in our school community!
